#### 1.) Q1 Sales Analysis Capstone Project
Create an Excel sales analysis dashboard to identify key performance indicators (KPIs) in quarter 1 sales for AdventureWorks- a bicycle parts shop and more. 

- Scope: Part order periods within 3 months (January, February, July).

Answer key questions regarding Q1 sales such as:

1.) Summarizing sales by product category and finding the total revenues by product category to check which product type receives the most sales. 
2.) Analyze monthly sales trends to understand which month had the largest peak for sales.
3.) Comparing the profitability of products by comparing the total revenue before taxes and after taxes, to review which products are the most profitable. 
4.) Identify best-selling products by checking which products are being sold in the highest quantity. 
5.) Understand shipping procedures and payment options used.
